Besides all the standards, the button reacts on cut & paste
and the long Delete/Remove Key press.

To match the Button to the corresponding Inputfield (Text) or Textarea
just add to the tag of the button or div the attribute for with the id-value of the Button. 

    <input type="text" id="input_id" />
    <button id="clear" for="input_id"></button>
